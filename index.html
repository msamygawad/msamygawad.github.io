Moh. Samy Afifi

Model-Based Design Eng.


An engineer with a solid technical foundation in robotic system design, control algorithms, and sensor-actuator integration, gained through hands-on projects.
I’ve worked on developing systems such as 3D printers, PCB CNC machines, laser cutters, hot wire CNCs, autonomous vehicles, and specialized devices like neck coolers, solar cell cleaning robots, OMR grading machines, vending machines, and can crushers. I led a team in building a Cube Satellite platform, focusing on deployment mechanisms.
As a teaching assistant, I guided students in embedded systems and contributed to academic research, publishing on optimal fractional order PID control. Currently, I am a Model-Based Design Engineer at Valeo, applying my experties on thermal system applications in the automotive industry.Get In Touch

3U- Cube Satelite

A three-unit CubeSat with a mission to record the Earth's surface. My team was responsible for developing the mechanical structure, as well as the deployment mechanisms for antennas and solar panels. The project objective was to create a fully functional CubeSat for destructive testing on Earth, using the data to develop a launch-ready model.

BurnWire Foam Machine

Development of the mechanical design and tightening mechanism of a burn-wire CNC machine dedicated to cutting foam into specific shapes for rifle gun suppressors.
